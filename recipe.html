Spaghetti Bolognese

A timeless Italian classic — rich, hearty, and full of flavor. This dish combines tender pasta with a savory tomato-based sauce, making it a favorite for family dinners.

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 250g ground beef
  • 400g canned tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • Fresh basil leaves (optional, for garnish)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pan, heat olive oil. Sauté the onion and garlic until fragrant and golden.
  3. Add the ground beef and cook until browned, breaking it apart with a spoon.
  4. Stir in the canned tomatoes, tomato paste, and oregano. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Reduce heat and let the sauce simmer for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Serve the sauce over the spaghetti. Garnish with fresh basil and Parmesan cheese.

Nutrition Facts (per serving)

Nutrient Amount
Calories 420 kcal
